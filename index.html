Xiaotian Jin

PhD Candidate in Statistics, KAUST

Hi, I am Xiaotian Jin, a PhD candidate in Statistics at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST), in the Stochastic Processes and Applied Statistics group, advised by David Bolin.

My research focus is hierarchical modeling, non-Gaussian processes, Bayesian inference, computational statistics, and optimization methods.

Previously, I completed my M.S. in Statistics at KAUST and my B.S. in Computer Science (minor in Mathematics) at Wenzhou-Kean University.

I am also an open-source developer of the ngme2 R package for non-Gaussian statistical modeling.
